Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Transaction data (amounts, recipients, timestamps)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate the Service, process transactions, maintain the blockchain ledger, comply with legal obligations, and improve the platform. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase Authentication and Firestore (Google Cloud Platform, US regions). Transaction data is recorded on the BradLedger blockchain, which is publicly verifiable but does not include personal identifiable information beyond UIDs.

4. M-PESA Data

When you use M-PESA services, your phone number is transmitted to Safaricom's Daraja API solely for processing deposits and withdrawals. We do not store your M-PESA PIN.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies for Firebase Authentication session management. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us at bradleynyadimo@gmail.com for requests.

8. Third-Party Services

BradPay uses Firebase (Google) for authentication and database services, and Safaricom Daraja API for M-PESA integration. These services have their own privacy policies.

9. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS, SHA-256 hashing, Firebase security rules) to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure.

10. Contact

For privacy inquiries: bradleynyadimo@gmail.com
