Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve the Service:

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, phone number, and a hashed PIN
  • Transaction Data: Amounts, timestamps, counterparties, and transaction references for all transfers, deposits, and withdrawals
  • M-PESA Data: Phone numbers used for deposits/withdrawals and M-PESA transaction IDs
  • Device & Usage Data: Browser type, device type, IP address, and app interaction data
  • Blockchain Data: All transactions are recorded on the BradLedger blockchain, which is publicly verifiable

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and settle transactions
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Maintain the BradLedger blockchain ledger
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Improve and debug the Service

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Safaricom (M-PESA): Phone numbers and amounts for deposit/withdrawal processing
  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and Firestore database hosting
  • Africa's Talking: Phone numbers for USSD session routing (if using BradUSSD)
  • Law Enforcement: If required by Kenyan law or a valid court order

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Firebase Authentication for secure identity management
  • SHA-256 hashing for the BradLedger blockchain
  • Transport Layer Security (TLS) for all API communications
  • PIN-based transaction authorization (PINs are hashed, never stored in plaintext)

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Transaction records on the BradLedger blockchain are permanent and cannot be deleted. You may request deletion of your account data (excluding blockchain records) by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to blockchain permanence)
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

7. Cookies

BradPay does not use tracking cookies. Firebase Authentication may use local storage tokens for session management.

8. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication & database
  • Safaricom M-PESA Daraja API — Payment processing
  • Africa's Talking — USSD gateway (optional)

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

9. Children's Privacy

BradPay is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be notified via email or in-app notice.

11. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@bradpay.co.ke.
